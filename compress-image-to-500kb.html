Why target 500KB?

500KB is a practical limit for website content images and email attachments. It is small enough to load quickly while preserving more detail than aggressive 20KB or 50KB compression.

FAQ

Is 500KB good for website images?

It is a reasonable upper limit for many content images. For hero images and Core Web Vitals, smaller is often better.

Can I keep JPG output?

Yes. Choose JPEG in settings if your workflow requires JPG compatibility.
