50KB is a strict target

A 50KB limit often requires both compression and smaller dimensions. This tool finds the best compression quality it can, but very large photos may need resizing first.

FAQ

Can every image reach 50KB?

No. Detailed large images may remain above 50KB at the lowest practical quality. Resize the image smaller, then compress again.

Is this safe for ID photos?

Yes for privacy: the image stays in your browser. Check the receiving form's required format before choosing WebP or JPG.
