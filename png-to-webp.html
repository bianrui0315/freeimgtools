Why convert PNG to WebP?

25–35% smaller files. WebP compresses better than PNG while maintaining the same visual quality. This means faster page loads and lower bandwidth usage.

Transparency preserved. Unlike JPG, WebP supports alpha transparency — logos and icons convert perfectly.

Google recommends it. Google's Core Web Vitals and PageSpeed Insights recommend WebP as the preferred image format for web.

Universal browser support. WebP is supported by Chrome, Firefox, Safari 14+, and Edge — covering 95%+ of web users.
