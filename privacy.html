Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

🔒

Short version: we collect nothing

Your images are processed locally in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your image files. No account required. No tracking beyond basic, anonymized analytics.

Image processing

All compression, conversion, and resizing operations run entirely inside your web browser using the Web Canvas API and JavaScript. Your image files are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party server.

When you use the AI Alt Text feature, your image is temporarily sent to Cloudflare's edge network for AI inference. Cloudflare processes the image in memory and returns a result — no image data is stored or retained by Cloudflare.

Analytics

We may use anonymized, aggregated analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-preserving and GDPR-compliant) to understand page usage patterns. This does not involve tracking individual users or storing personal information.

Advertising

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing activity. You can opt out of personalized ads at adssettings.google.com. We do not share any personal data with Google beyond what is standard for AdSense operation.

Cookies

We do not set first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies for ad personalization.

Data retention

Because we do not receive or store your image files, there is no image data to retain. Any anonymized analytics data is retained for up to 90 days.

Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
